From.9

From.9 is the first and special single album of the South Korean girl group, Fromis 9. The album was released on October 10, 2018 by Off the Record Entertainment and distributed by Stone Music Entertainment and Genie Music. The album's lead single is "Love Bomb".

From.9 marks the first comeback of the group with Jang Gyu-ri after her absence due to participating in Produce 48.
